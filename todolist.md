### 1. Privacy Policy

- **Purpose**
  - Explains how you handle data (cookies, analytics, ads).
  - Required by Google AdSense and most ad networks.
- **Must include**
  - What data you collect (IP, cookies, analytics).
  - Third-party services (Google Analytics, AdSense).
  - User rights (opt-out, delete data).
- **Action**
  - Create `/privacy.html`
  - Link it in the footer.

### 2. Terms & Conditions (REQUIRED)

- **Purpose**
  - Protects you legally.
  - Very important because this is a **lye calculator (safety tool)**.
- **Must include**
  - “For educational purposes only”
  - No liability for misuse or incorrect results
  - User responsibility for safety and verification
- **Action**
  - Create `/terms.html`
  - Strong disclaimer about **NaOH handling**.
